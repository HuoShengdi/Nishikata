Nishikata

Spirits ousted from their former home in the Far East came to reside in a secluded village in the American West. Alas, all was not quiet. Defend your new home from would-be invaders!

How to Play

Dodge enemy fire, and shoot down enemies to progress. Watch out for the boss!

Start Game

Controls

↑ ↓ : move up and down
← → : move left and right
Z : fire weapon
Shift : hold to move slower

Game Over

Play Again 
Arrow keys: move     Z: fire     Shift: slow movement
